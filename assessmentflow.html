Assess Your Proficiency: Business Analyst

Honest self-assessment is the first step to building your roadmap. Evaluate your current capabilities against the Ten10 framework below.

Competency Level Key

  • 1 - Novice: Fundamental awareness, requires close supervision.
  • 2 - Practitioner: Can perform routine tasks independently.
  • 3 - Competent: Handles complex issues, works fully independently.
  • 4 - Advanced: Deep expertise, guides others, optimizes processes.
  • 5 - Expert: Sets organizational standards and strategic direction.

Core Technical Skills

Requirements Engineering
Expertise in capturing User Stories, Acceptance Criteria, and Non-Functional Requirements.
Data Visualization
Proficiency in Power BI or Tableau to present data-driven insights to stakeholders.
Process Modelling
Advanced use of BPMN 2.0 or UML for "As-Is" and "To-Be" mapping.

Soft Skills

Stakeholder Management
Navigating complex corporate hierarchies to achieve consensus.
Facilitation & Discovery
Running high-energy discovery workshops (hybrid/remote environments).
